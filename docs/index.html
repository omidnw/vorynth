How it works

Two parts, one purpose: collect what matters and put it in front of you — all on your machine.

Vorynth ├── Desktop App The window you open. Beautiful, fast, shows your briefs and search results. └── Core Engine Runs in the background — collects from your sources, summarizes, and indexes everything for search. How data flows 1. You add sources (blogs, journals, news sites, any RSS feed). 2. The engine checks them automatically, on your schedule. 3. AI reads each article, classifies it, and builds your daily brief. 4. Everything is indexed — search or ask questions across all your sources. 5. The desktop app shows your briefs, search results, and insights. And none of it leaves your computer.
